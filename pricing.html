Pricing

If We Don't Recover Your Money, You Owe Us Absolutely Nothing!!

When We Do Collect, DCWs Contingency Fee Is The Following:

  • 30% Of The Amount Collected On Accounts Placed Under One Year Of Age.
  • 40% Of The Amount Collected On Accounts Placed Over One Year Of Age.
  • 50% Of The Amount Collected On Accounts Placed Under $1,000.
  • 50% Of The Amount Collected On Accounts Placed With An Attorney.

If You Decide To Proceed With Legal Action, You Will Be Required To Pay The Upfront Legal Costs, Such As Court Costs, Filing Fees, Etc... These Fees Vary Depending On The Debtor's Jurisdiction And The Details Of Each Individual Case. Upon Payment Of These Funds, Our Affiliated Attorney Will File A Lawsuit On Your Behalf For All Monies Owed, Including, But Not Limited To, The Costs To File This Action. If Our Attempts To Collect Via Litigation Fail, The Case Will Be Closed. You Will Owe Nothing To Our Firm Or Our Affiliated Attorney.
